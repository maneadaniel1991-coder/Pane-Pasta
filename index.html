From Italian kitchens to London offices.

After years cooking in Italian kitchens and running high-volume restaurants in London, we wanted to strip things back to what really matters: honest recipes, proper technique, and food that still tastes like Italy when it reaches your desk.

Pane & Pasta starts as a dark kitchen in Hammersmith, focusing on office deliveries and corporate catering. Every sauce, every tray of focaccia and every portion of fresh pasta is made from scratch by people who grew up with these recipes.

What you’ll be able to order.

Fresh Pasta

Tagliatelle, gnocchi and more, paired with slow-cooked ragù, creamy sauces and seasonal specials. Built to arrive al dente, not soggy.

Pane from Puglia

Thick, airy focaccia baked in trays, sliced and filled with salumi, burrata, grilled veg or kept simple with olive oil & rosemary.

Dolci & Extras

A small but mighty dessert list and rotating extras – from tiramisù to seasonal soups for cold London days.

We’ll shape the menu with feedback from our first customers, adding monthly specials and seasonal dishes as we grow.

Office lunches & corporate catering.

Pane & Pasta is built with teams in mind – from regular office lunches to larger meetings and events. We’ll offer sharing trays, set menus and custom options for partners who order frequently.
