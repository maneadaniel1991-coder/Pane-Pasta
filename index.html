From Italian kitchens to London offices.

After years cooking in Italian kitchens and running high-volume restaurants in London, we wanted to strip things back to what really matters: honest recipes, proper technique, and food that still tastes like Italy when it reaches your desk.

Pane & Pasta starts as a dark kitchen in Hammersmith, focusing on office deliveries and corporate catering. Every sauce, every tray of focaccia and every portion of fresh pasta is made from scratch by people who grew up with these recipes.

Two Italians, one very hungry city.

Pane & Pasta was born from two Italians who grew up with Nonna's kitchen as the centre of the house: a wooden table, a pot of sauce that never really left the stove, and people constantly arriving hungry. Moving to London, we saw how often food is rushed, over-complicated or made far away from the kitchen that serves it.

Our dream is simple: take the kind of food our Nonne would proudly put on the Sunday table - slow-simmered ragu, fluffy focaccia and bowls of pasta that actually fill you - and make it reachable on a normal lunch break, a Tuesday night on the sofa or a weekend with friends.

We're starting in a dark kitchen in Hammersmith so we can focus on cooking instead of decor. We'll deliver to offices, co-working spaces, homes and hotels in the area - anyone who wants real Italian comfort food without the fuss.

  • Real ingredients, cooked in small batches.
  • Menus that change with the seasons.
  • Food that feels homemade, even from a delivery bag.

How we're rolling out Pane & Pasta.

We're opening step by step so we can listen, improve and grow with the neighbourhood. From offices to homes, our launch journey is designed to keep things personal and under control.

Step 1 - Behind the scenes

Testing, tasting & fine-tuning.

We're finalising our dark kitchen in Hammersmith, perfecting sauces, pasta shapes and focaccia dough, testing packaging and talking to local offices, co-working spaces and neighbours.

Step 2 - Soft launch

Limited hours, full flavour.

We'll start with a focused menu and limited delivery hours so we can stay close to every order. Expect office lunches, shared trays for meetings and individual deliveries to nearby homes and hotels.

Step 3 - Full service & growth

More hours, more dishes, more people fed.

Once the foundations are solid, we'll extend our hours, add seasonal dishes and build long-term catering partnerships - while staying open to anyone in the area who simply wants a bowl of proper pasta or a slice of warm focaccia.

Office lunches & corporate catering.

Pane & Pasta is built with teams in mind – from regular office lunches to larger meetings and events. We’ll offer sharing trays, set menus and custom options for partners who order frequently.
